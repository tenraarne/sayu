I asked Mats
”What is our spirit animal?
A tiger, a bear?”

He was silent for a long time, turned to me with a smile on his face and said
”The Earth Worm!”
Yes we are!
Preferably invisible.
Bettering the soil for architecture.
Our images are propagators, fertilizers, ambassadors.

Our work is about empowering architects by creating enthusiasm.
Not about us.
Images are myths.
Still there when the city is dust.
